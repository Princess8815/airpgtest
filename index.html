Arcstone Legends

Rebuild the shattered realm using every skill you can muster. Explore, gather, craft, quest, and challenge bosses with tactical combat styles.

Move: WASD Interact: E Switch style: 1 Melee · 2 Ranged · 3 Mage

Prologue

The beacon-city of Arcstone fell when the Ashen Wraith shattered the world rune. The surviving sentinels sealed fragments across the valley. You must reunite them, restore the rune, and face the Wraith before its legion consumes the last light.

Every skill matters: harvest to rebuild the camp, craft gear, fletch ammunition, cook for healing, pray to strengthen spirit, and forge runes to channel magic.
