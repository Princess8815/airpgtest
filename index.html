Menu-first 2D RPG prototype

Arcstone Legends

Travel via NPC caravans, gather and craft gear tiers, and clear bosses through tactical style swapping. Everything is menu-driven so you can read the story and know exactly what to do.

Travel: choose a destination below Combat: choose a target, press Space or click "Strike" Styles: 1 Melee · 2 Ranged (arrows) · 3 Magic (runes)

Prologue

The Ashen Wraith shattered the world rune. The sentinels sealed the shards across the valley. Find allies, forge tiered gear, claim the Rune of Dawn, and confront the Wraith in the Ashen Spire.

Every skill matters: gather, smith bronze → iron → steel, fletch ammunition, cook for sustain, craft sigils, pray for wards, and runecraft for magic.
